I photograph small worlds at sunset.

I'm Nitish — a self-taught macro photographer drawn to the kind of moments most people walk past. An ant cresting a leaf. A droplet holding the entire sky. A flower folding into bokeh.

Most of these were shot in the last hour of light, often within a few feet of my home. I believe small subjects deserve large attention.
